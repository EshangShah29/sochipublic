Welcome to SOCHI!

SOCHI is a UMSI tech consulting club helping students gain hands-on experience in consulting. Through workshops and project teams working directly with businesses, we create a supportive space for professional growth and meaningful impact.

Whether you're exploring consulting for the first time or looking to sharpen your skills, SOCHI has something for you, including hands-on tech consulting experience that can set you apart in internship recruiting.

Looking forward to what you’ll build with us,
